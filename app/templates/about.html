About InterViewPoint

Welcome to InterViewPoint, your dedicated platform designed to empower students in their placement journeys. We understand the challenges of navigating interviews and preparing for a successful career. Our mission is to foster a collaborative community where students can learn from shared experiences.

Our Mission

To provide a centralized and accessible repository of interview experiences, helping students gain practical insights into various company recruitment processes and roles. We aim to reduce the anxiety associated with placements by offering transparent, peer-contributed information.

What We Offer

  • Real Interview Experiences: Read detailed accounts from students who've been through the hiring process with various companies.
  • Community Contribution: A platform for students to anonymously share their own interview journeys, contributing to a growing knowledge base.
  • Admin Vetted Content: All submitted experiences are reviewed by administrators to ensure quality, relevance, and appropriateness.
  • Simplified Access: A clean and intuitive interface focused solely on interview preparation resources.

Our Vision

To become the go-to resource for college students seeking career guidance and interview preparation, built on the foundation of shared knowledge and community support.
